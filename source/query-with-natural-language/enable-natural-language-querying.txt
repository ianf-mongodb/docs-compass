================================
Enable Natural Language Querying
================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1

You can enable Compass natural language querying by toggling the 
:guilabel:`AI Query Generator` setting. Natural language queries can 
be a helpful starting point and assist you in learning to write MongoDB 
queries.

About this Task
---------------

Alternatively, you can click the :guilabel:`Log in to Atlas to enable` button 
within the :guilabel:`Use natural language to generate queries and 
pipelines` modal. This modal displays after updating Compass to a 
version that supports natural language querying.

Before you Begin
----------------

To enable Compass natural language querying you must connect to an 
Atlas deployment to. For details on how to get a free Atlas account and 
deploy a free M0 cluster, see :ref:`atlas-getting-started`.
