================
Compass Settings
================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

|Compass-short| has various settings that can be set to help improve
your user experience. 

To view the |Compass-short| :guilabel:`Settings`, on the top menu bar
of |Compass-short| :

.. procedure::
   :style: normal

   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Help`.

      .. figure:: /images/settings/settings-click-help.png
         :alt: Click Help

   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Settings`.

      .. figure:: /images/settings/settings-click-settings.png
         :alt: Click Settings

      The settings menu appears.

      .. figure:: /images/settings/settings-menu.png
         :alt: Settings menu

The :guilabel:`Settings` menu has three types of settings:

- General, used to disable or enable specific features.
- Theme, used to enable or disable dark mode.
- Privacy, used to enable or disable external network requests.
