===============
Theme Settings
===============

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

The :guilabel:`Theme` pane allows you to enable or disable dark theme 
mode.

To enable dark theme mode:

.. procedure::
   :style: normal

   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Dark theme`.

      .. figure:: /images/settings/settings-theme-click-darkmode.png
         :alt: Click dark mode

   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Save`.

      .. figure:: /images/settings/theme-click-save.png
         :alt: Click Save
